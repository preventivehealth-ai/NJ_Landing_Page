For Individuals
Why this exists

This company was born out of my own health journey.

In August 2015, at the age of 43, I was diagnosed with cardiovascular disease. The years that followed were a deliberate rebuild of my health and learning to advocate for myself — blood markers, body composition scans, wearables, genomics, and the willingness to act on what the evidence actually said. My visceral fat reduced from 10 to 2, I gained 12% of muscle mass, increased my bone density, and turned a fragile cardiovascular profile into a resilient one. My cardiovascular age is now at −15 years, my biological age is 12 years lower than my chronological age, and more importantly, I get to continue enjoying competitive soccer at 54, with the hope of continuing it for as long as possible. PreventiveHealth.ai exists to give every person — and every clinician — the tools and the partnership to do this earlier, with less guesswork, and with more confidence.

— Nickhil Jakatdar, Ph.D.Founder · PreventiveHealth.ai
